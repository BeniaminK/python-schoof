Detailed Description

Auxiliary functions to handle congruences and quotient classes.

The algorithms and implementations are kept as simple as possible. They are not meant for high performance computing, but for instructive purposes.

Author
Peter Dinges pding.nosp@m.es@a.nosp@m.cm.or.nosp@m.g

Return the unique representative of quotient_class in valid_range.

For example, the representative of (3 mod 7) in range(13, 19) is 17.

Parameters

Returns
